           similar passage. Great precision, low breadth.

           Without FAISS to anchor it, too many tangentially related chunks surface.

           The 0.7/0.3 fusion adds targeted breadth without killing precision.
           For multi-hop questions spread nearly triples (65 -> 182) while
           maintaining keyword coverage — the graph pulls in the second section
           needed to construct a complete answer.
